About Me

Born in Philippines, and forever consumed with answering the question: Why is that compelling? Taking pictures has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. My work is an obsession, so every day and every shoot is an opportunity to learn and to further develop my craft - discovering more about myself, and my fellow earthlings.

My portfolio showcases some favorite images from the past few years; I strive to create photographs that not only capture a moment, but also capture a feeling and essence.

I'd love to hear from you, so feel free to contact me.

Thank you for taking time to look at my site and work; I hope to meet you in the near future.
